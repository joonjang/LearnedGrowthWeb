Adversity

My friend read my message but didn't reply for hours.

Belief

They find me annoying. Nobody likes me. I always say the wrong thing.

Thinking Pattern Observed
Time Permanent
Speech implies this "Always" happens.
Scope Everything
Belief leaks the situation into everything.
Blame Personal
Internalizes cause ("I'm annoying").
Alternative Perspective
"A delayed reply is usually about their schedule, not your personality. Friends often get busy; it doesn't mean the friendship is over."
