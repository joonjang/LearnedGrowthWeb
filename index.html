Adversity

My friend read my message but didn't reply for hours.

Belief

They find me annoying. Nobody likes me. I always say the wrong thing.

Thinking Pattern Observed
Time Permanent
Speech implies this "Always" happens.
Scope Everything
Belief leaks the situation into everything.
Blame Personal
Internalizes cause ("I'm annoying").
Alternative Perspective
"A delayed reply is usually about their schedule, not your personality. Friends often get busy; it doesn't mean the friendship is over."

How it Works

A simple loop to break negative thinking habits.

1. Catch

Capture the moment. Write down what happened and what you told yourself about it.

2. Check

Our AI recognizes patterns in your speech and guides you to ask better internal questions, helping you challenge your assumptions.

3. Change

Challenge the assumption. Transform the negative thought into a clearer perspective.
