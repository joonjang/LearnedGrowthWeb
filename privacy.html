Privacy Policy

App: LearnedGrowth (“Learned Growth”, “we”, “us”)
Website: learnedgrowth.com
Last Updated: January 8, 2026

LearnedGrowth is a journaling app inspired by the ABCDE steps from Learned Optimism . This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Plain-English note: Your journal content can be personal. We treat it with care. We don’t show ads, and we don’t sell your personal information.

1) What we collect

A. Account information

  • Email address (for one-time passcode (OTP) login and account access)
  • Apple Sign-In / Google Sign-In identifiers (if you choose those login methods)

B. Journal content you choose to save

  • Your entries and responses related to Adversity (A) , Belief (B) , Consequence (C) , and optionally Disputation (D) and Energization (E)
  • Optional tags, titles, and any notes you add

C. Purchase and subscription information

  • Subscription status (e.g., active/inactive), entitlement/access level, and purchase receipts needed to verify purchases
  • We do not collect or store your full payment card details. Payments are processed by Apple App Store / Google Play (and our subscription/purchase tooling).

D. App/device and diagnostics information

  • Device and app information (e.g., app version, device type, operating system version)
  • Logs needed to keep the app working (crash reports, error logs, performance diagnostics)

E. Analytics / tracking (current state)

We do not run in-app ads and we do not intentionally use third-party remarketing services. We may receive aggregated analytics from platform providers (for example, Google Play or Apple) or from diagnostics services that help us understand app reliability.

If we add additional analytics tools in the future, we will update this policy and, where required, offer choices/consent.

2) How we use your information

  • Provide the service (create your account, sign you in, sync your entries)
  • Send OTP login codes to your email (and only for login/account access)
  • Run AI analysis if you request it (see Section 3)
  • Verify subscriptions/consumables and enforce usage limits (including fair-use limits)
  • Security and fraud prevention (protect accounts and prevent abuse)
  • App reliability (debugging, crash fixes, performance improvements)
  • Legal compliance (tax/accounting records as required, responding to lawful requests)

3) AI analysis (how it works)

If you choose to use AI features (typically available via subscription or consumable credits), we send the text you request to analyze (for example, your A/B/C entry) to our AI service provider(s) so they can generate an analysis.

  • You control what you submit to AI analysis.
  • Not a therapist: AI outputs are for self-reflection and are not medical or professional advice.
  • Provider handling: AI providers may process data according to their own policies. We aim to minimize what we send and how long it’s retained, where configuration options allow.

Tip: Avoid putting extremely sensitive identifiers into your journal (e.g., government ID numbers).

4) Where your information is stored

  • Cloud storage: We store your account and journal data in our database so you can sync across devices.
  • Service providers: We use infrastructure providers to host the backend and deliver AI features.

5) Who we share information with

We share information only as needed to operate LearnedGrowth:

  • Authentication providers: Apple and Google (if you choose those login methods)
  • Email delivery provider(s): to send OTP login codes
  • Cloud database/backend provider(s): for secure storage and sync
  • AI provider(s): only when you request AI analysis
  • App stores: Apple and Google for purchase processing and verification
  • Legal: if required by law, or to protect rights/safety

6) Data retention

We keep your information for as long as needed to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

  • Journal entries: retained until you delete them or delete your account (subject to backups and legal requirements)
  • OTP emails: used for login and not for marketing
  • Purchase records: retained as required for accounting and compliance

7) Your choices and controls

  • Edit/delete entries: You can edit or delete journal content inside the app.
  • AI features: You can choose not to use AI analysis.
  • Account deletion: You can request account deletion (see Contact section).

8) Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect your data. No system can be 100% secure, but we work hard to protect your information.

9) Children’s privacy

LearnedGrowth is not intended for children under 13 . We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

10) International users

If you use LearnedGrowth outside Canada, your information may be processed in countries where our service providers operate. We take steps designed to protect your information consistent with this policy and applicable law.
